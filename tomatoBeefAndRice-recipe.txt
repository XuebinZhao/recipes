Ingredients:
 - 1 pound lean ground beef (90% lean)
 - 3 cups canned diced tomatoes, undrained
 - 1 medium green pepper, chopped
 - 1 cup uncooked long grain rice
 - 1 teaspoon chili powder
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
 - 1/4 teaspoon pepper

Directions:
 1. In a large bowl, combine all ingredients. Place in a grease 2-qt. baking dish. Cover and bake at 400 degree for 1 - 1/2 hours, stirring once or twice. Uncover during the last 15 minutes to brown.

Yield: 6 servings.
